Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What is ZapCodes?

ZapCodes is an AI-powered platform that lets you build professional websites and web applications without writing code. Just describe what you want, and our AI builds it for you.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes! All plans include custom domain support with free SSL certificates. Simply point your domain to our servers and you're live.

Is there a free trial?

Absolutely. Every plan comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. Cancel anytime during the trial and you won't be charged.

Can I export my code?

Yes! You own your code. Export clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript at any time. No vendor lock-in.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
